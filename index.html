About Prepegola

Built On Trust,
Driven By Excellence

PREPEGOLA BUILDERS LIMITED is a professional building and construction company committed to delivering quality, precision, and excellence in every project. We specialize in building construction, architectural design, roofing solutions, and general construction services for residential, commercial, and institutional clients.

With a strong focus on innovation, functionality, and durability, we work closely with our clients from concept development to project completion, ensuring every structure meets the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.

How We Work

Our Construction Process

01

Initial Consultation

Comprehensive discussion of your vision, requirements, budget and timeline. Site assessment and goal setting before anything begins.

02

Planning & Feasibility

Detailed project plan, cost estimates and bill of quantities. Soil tests, site surveys and regulatory compliance checks.

03

Architectural Design

2D plans, 3D visualisations and structural drawings reviewed and approved by you before construction begins.

04

Construction Execution

Certified engineers and skilled tradespeople execute the build with precision. Regular site updates and photos at every milestone.

05

Quality Inspection

Multi-stage quality audits at structural, roofing and finishing phases. No stage proceeds until every standard is met.

06

Handover & Support

Project handed over with full documentation, warranties and a post-construction support period. We stand behind our work.
